OUR STORY OF RELIABILITY

In 1975, a young carpenter named Don started his journey in Chicago's flooring industry with a simple principle: show up on time and deliver quality work. That principle became the foundation of what would grow into SupplySide Flooring Installation Chicago.

For nearly five decades, Don has perfected his craft, installing floors in thousands of Chicago homes and businesses. His dedication to reliability and craftsmanship earned him a reputation as one of Chicago's most trusted flooring professionals.

Today, SupplySide Flooring is proudly owned and operated by Matt Lee, who brings fresh energy and modern business practices while maintaining Don's time-tested values. Together with Don as our lead installer, we combine old-school craftsmanship with contemporary customer service.

"In this business, your word is everything. When we say we'll be there at 8 AM, we're there at 8 AM. When we quote a job, that's the price. No surprises, no excuses."

- Don, Lead Installer (48 years experience)
