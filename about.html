About SupplySide Flooring

Four decades of trust, reliability, and exceptional craftsmanship

Our Story: Built on Trust Since 1984

When Matt Lee started SupplySide Flooring Installation over 40 years ago, he had one simple philosophy: "Show up on time and do what you say you'll do." In an industry plagued by unreliable contractors and hidden costs, this straightforward approach set us apart.

What began as a one-man operation in Chicago's South Side has grown into one of the city's most trusted flooring installation companies. But despite our growth, our core values haven't changed. We still answer every call personally, provide honest quotes, and treat every home like it's our own.

Today, Matt and his partner Don lead a team that has installed flooring in thousands of Chicago homes and businesses. From historic brownstones in Lincoln Park to modern condos in the Loop, we've earned our reputation one floor at a time.

"In 40 years, I've learned that quality work speaks for itself. But what really matters to homeowners is knowing they can trust you to show up, do the job right, and stand behind your work. That's what we do every single day."
- Matt Lee, Founder
