Natural Stone Installation FAQs

How much does natural stone installation cost in Chicago? +

Natural stone installation in Chicago typically ranges from $8-25 per square foot, depending on the stone type and installation complexity. Marble and exotic stones command premium prices, while slate and travertine offer luxury at more accessible price points. Our average project runs $15,000, including materials and expert installation.

Can natural stone handle Chicago's freeze-thaw cycles? +

Yes, when properly installed and sealed. We use specialized installation techniques and sealers designed for Chicago's climate. Granite and slate excel in freeze-thaw conditions, while softer stones like marble require more careful placement away from exterior entrances.

How long does stone floor installation take? +

A typical Chicago home installation takes 3-7 days, depending on square footage and complexity. This includes subfloor preparation, stone setting, grouting, and sealing. Larger projects or intricate patterns may require additional time. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

What maintenance does natural stone require? +

Natural stone is surprisingly low-maintenance with proper sealing. Daily care involves dust mopping and weekly damp mopping with pH-neutral cleaners. We recommend professional resealing every 2-3 years for Chicago homes to protect against salt and moisture. We provide complete care instructions specific to your stone type.

Is natural stone slippery when wet? +

Different stones offer varying slip resistance. Textured slate and flamed granite provide excellent traction, while polished marble requires more caution. We recommend specific finishes for Chicago entryways and bathrooms to ensure safety without sacrificing beauty.

Can you install stone over existing flooring? +

Sometimes, but it depends on the existing floor condition and your home's structure. Chicago's older homes often require subfloor reinforcement for heavy stone. We evaluate each situation individually and recommend the best approach for lasting results.
