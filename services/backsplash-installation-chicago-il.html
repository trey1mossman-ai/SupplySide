Backsplash Installation FAQs

How much does backsplash installation cost in Chicago? +

Average Chicago backsplash installations range from $1,500-4,000, with most projects around $2,500. This includes materials and professional installation. Costs vary based on material choice, kitchen size, and design complexity. We provide free, detailed quotes.

Can you really install a backsplash in one day? +

Yes! Most Chicago kitchen backsplashes complete in 4-6 hours. Standard 30-50 square foot installations finish same-day. Larger or complex designs may require 2 days. We minimize disruption – you can use your kitchen the same evening.

Do I need to move out during installation? +

No! Backsplash installation is minimally invasive. We protect your Chicago kitchen and work cleanly. You can remain home, though we recommend avoiding the kitchen during installation. Most homeowners are cooking dinner in their updated kitchen the same night.

What's the best backsplash height? +

Standard height is 18 inches from countertop to bottom of upper cabinets. However, many Chicago homeowners now prefer full-height backsplashes extending to the ceiling for dramatic effect. We'll help you choose based on your kitchen's proportions and style.

Can you install backsplash over existing tile? +

Sometimes, but we typically recommend removal for best results. Installing over existing tile can create thickness issues with outlets and switches. Our Chicago team evaluates each situation and recommends the best approach for lasting results.

How do I maintain my new backsplash? +

Most backsplashes require minimal maintenance. Regular wiping with mild soap and water keeps them beautiful. We seal natural stone and provide specific care instructions. Chicago's hard water may require occasional calcium deposit removal with vinegar solution.
