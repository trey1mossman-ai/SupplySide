Carpet Tile Installation FAQs

How long does carpet tile installation take for a typical Chicago office? +

Most Chicago office installations can be completed over a weekend. A typical 5,000 square foot office takes 1-2 days, while larger spaces may require 3-4 days. We work around your schedule to minimize business disruption.

What's the cost difference between carpet tiles and traditional carpet? +

While carpet tiles typically cost $3-7 per square foot installed in Chicago, they often prove more economical long-term. The ability to replace individual tiles rather than entire sections can save thousands in maintenance costs over the carpet's lifetime.

Can carpet tiles handle Chicago's winter salt and moisture? +

Yes! Quality carpet tiles are designed for commercial use and handle moisture better than traditional carpet. We recommend entry mats and can install moisture-resistant tiles in high-traffic entry areas to protect against Chicago's harsh winters.

Do you move furniture during installation? +

Yes, our experienced Chicago crew handles furniture moving as part of our service. We use professional equipment to protect your assets and can work in phases to keep parts of your office operational during installation.

What brands of carpet tiles do you install? +

We install all major commercial carpet tile brands including Interface, Shaw, Mohawk, and Milliken. As Chicago's experienced installers, we can recommend the best options for your specific needs and budget.

How do carpet tiles perform in high-traffic Chicago buildings? +

Commercial-grade carpet tiles are specifically designed for high traffic. With proper installation and maintenance, they last 10-15 years in busy Chicago offices. Individual tile replacement extends this lifespan even further.
