Why Choose Tile Flooring?

The perfect choice for Chicago kitchens and bathrooms

100% Waterproof

Perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, and basements. Tile won't absorb water, preventing mold and damage even in Chicago's humid summers.

Endless Design Options

From classic subway tile to modern large format, wood-look to marble-look. Create any style from traditional Chicago bungalow to contemporary loft.

Extremely Durable

Porcelain tile is harder than granite. It won't scratch, dent, or stain. Perfect for high-traffic areas and active families.

Easy to Clean

Simple sweep and mop maintenance. Stain-resistant and doesn't harbor bacteria. Ideal for kitchens and bathrooms.

Radiant Heat Compatible

Excellent heat conductor for radiant floor heating. Keep your feet warm during Chicago's cold winters while saving on heating costs.

Increases Home Value

Quality tile installation is a strong selling point. Buyers love the durability and low maintenance of tile in wet areas.

Our Tile Installation Process

Precision craftsmanship at every step

1

Design Consultation

We help you select the perfect tile, discuss layout patterns, and plan your project. We'll bring samples and show you design options that work with your space and style.

2

Surface Preparation

Proper prep is crucial for lasting results. We ensure surfaces are level, install waterproof membranes where needed, and create the perfect substrate for your tile.

Precision Installation

Using laser levels and years of expertise, we install each tile perfectly. Consistent spacing, level surfaces, and clean cuts around fixtures ensure professional results.

We apply color-matched grout with consistent lines, then seal everything for water resistance and easy maintenance. Final cleanup leaves your space ready to enjoy.

Tile Installation FAQs

Your tile questions answered

Porcelain is denser and less porous than ceramic, making it more water-resistant and durable. It's ideal for high-traffic areas and anywhere moisture is a concern. Ceramic is more affordable and easier to cut, perfect for walls and light-traffic areas. Both are excellent choices - we'll help you decide based on your specific needs.

A typical bathroom floor (50-75 sq ft) takes 2-3 days: one day for prep and installation, one day for grouting, and light use after 24 hours. Kitchen backsplashes usually take 1-2 days. Larger areas or complex patterns take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your estimate.

Porcelain and ceramic tiles don't need sealing, but grout does! We always seal grout to prevent staining and water absorption. Natural stone tiles require sealing. We use premium sealers and recommend resealing grout every 2-3 years for optimal protection.

Sometimes! Tile can often go over existing tile, vinyl, or concrete if the surface is level and well-bonded. We always inspect first - uneven surfaces need leveling, and some materials must be removed. Proper preparation ensures your new tile lasts decades without cracking or coming loose.

Surprisingly, larger tiles can make small spaces look bigger! Fewer grout lines create a cleaner, more spacious feel. We often recommend 12x24" or even 24x24" tiles for small Chicago bathrooms. Light colors and minimal grout contrast also help. We'll show you options during consultation.
