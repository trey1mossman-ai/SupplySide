Why Choose Hardwood Flooring?

Timeless beauty and lasting value for Chicago homes

Timeless Elegance

Nothing matches the warmth and beauty of real hardwood. Each plank is unique, creating character that improves with age and adds charm to any Chicago home.

Increases Home Value

Hardwood floors are a top selling point in Chicago real estate. They typically offer 70-80% ROI and make your home more attractive to buyers.

Lasts 100+ Years

With proper care, hardwood floors can last over a century. They can be refinished multiple times, making them a true lifetime investment.

Eco-Friendly Choice

Sustainably sourced hardwood is renewable and biodegradable. It improves indoor air quality and doesn't harbor allergens like carpet.

Refinish & Refresh

Unlike other flooring, hardwood can be sanded and refinished to look brand new. Change stain colors to match evolving styles without replacement.

Endless Options

Choose from oak, maple, cherry, walnut, and exotic species. Wide or narrow planks, different stains, and finish options create your perfect look.

Our Hardwood Installation Process

NWFA certified excellence from start to finish

1

Expert Consultation

We assess your space, test moisture levels, and help you select the perfect wood species, grade, and finish. Our 48 years of experience guides you to the best choice for Chicago's climate and your lifestyle.

2

Acclimation & Preparation

Wood must acclimate to your home's humidity for 3-7 days. We prepare the subfloor, ensuring it's level, dry, and structurally sound. Proper prep prevents future squeaks and gaps.

3

Precision Installation

Using NWFA best practices, we install your hardwood with proper expansion gaps, secure fastening, and perfect alignment. Each board is inspected for quality before installation.

4

Sanding & Finishing

For unfinished wood, we sand to perfection using dust-containment systems. Multiple coats of your chosen finish are applied, with proper drying time between each for maximum durability.

For pre-finished hardwood, you can walk on it immediately after installation. For site-finished floors, you can walk in socks after 24 hours, place furniture after 3-4 days, and return area rugs after 2 weeks. Full cure takes 30 days. We use quick-dry finishes when faster turnaround is needed.
